Mango Juice Recipe

Mango juice is one of my favorite drinks. It is sweet, refreshing, healthy, and easy to make at home. Fresh mango juice is especially enjoyable during the summer season.

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe mangoes
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons sugar (optional)
  • Ice cubes

Cooking Steps

  1. Wash and peel the mangoes.
  2. Cut the mangoes into small pieces.
  3. Put the mango pieces into a blender.
  4. Add milk,water, and sugar.
  5. Blend until smooth.
  6. Add ice cubes and serve chilled.

Extra Toppings and Side Items

  • Toppings
    • whipped cream
    • Mango slices
    • chocolate chips
    • Crushed ice
  • Side Items
    • Cookies
    • Fruit salad
    • Sandwiches
